Haikyuu!!

What is Haikyuu About?

Haikyu!! is an anime made by Haruichi Furudate.
The story follows Shoyo Hinata, a boy wanting to
become a great volleyball player despite his
small stature. The anime is all about
comedy and drama.

More about Haikyuu!!

An anime television series adaptation by Production I.G aired from
April 2014 to September 2014, with 25 episodes. A second season
aired from October 2015 to March 2016, with 25 episodes. A third
season aired from October 2016 to December 2016, with 10 episodes.
A fourth season was announced during the Jump Festa '19 and was
planned for release in two cours, the first cour of 13 episodes
aired from January to April 2020, and the second cour of 12
episodes aired from October to December 2020. You can find Haikyuu!!
on Dubbedanime5. Dubbedanime5 is an online website where you
can watch any anime in english.

The main Character in Haikyuu!!

The main Character is Shoyo Hinata! He is 16 and wants to become a great volleyball player.
Hinata has a cheerful and energetic personality. He is easily excited and can be quite loud, a trait
second only to Nishinoya and Tanaka. For the most part, Hinata is very simple-minded which
sometimes causes him to make rash decisions.
